ERASMUS+ advantages

Erasmus+ is a European Union (EU) program that supports education, training, youth, and sport in Europe. Launched in 2014, it builds on previous EU programs like Erasmus, which focused primarily on student exchange. Erasmus+ has a broader scope, offering opportunities for individuals and organizations in participating countries to engage in activities that enhance skills, employability, and intercultural understanding.

What is it?

Erasmus+ allows students in higher education to study or undertake internships abroad. This includes both intra-European exchanges and opportunities with partner countries outside Europe.

The program supports youth exchanges, allowing young people to spend time in another country to learn and engage in cultural activities. It also supports the European Voluntary Service (EVS), where young people can volunteer abroad.
